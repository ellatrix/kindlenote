This tiny app allows you to take notes in your Kindle’s Web Browser.

The note will be saved, so don’t worry about closing the browser or refreshing the page.

You can download the note as a text file, and then it will appear in your Kindle’s Library. When you connect your Kindle by USB, you can transfer the files.

Alternatively, you could simply take a picture of the note with your phone, and then select the text if your phone supports it.
